Faith Talk

The Fight of Faith

“We having the same spirit of faith, according as it is written, I believed, and therefore have I spoken; we also believe, and therefore speak" (2 Corinthians 4:13)

Too many people have the idea that if something is God’s will for them to have that it will just automatically happen without any effort or involvement on their part.

Faith Speaks

The spirit of faith speaks! (Hebrews 11:1-2) It calls things that be not as though they were.

“God’s Word is His covenant with His children. “…My Spirit is abiding in your midst; do not fear” (Paraphrased) Haggai 2:5

“if you say [the word of faith] to the mountain” of problems that is standing before you “it will be done.” (Matt 23:21). God himself stands as the guarantee to fulfil the word of faith that you speak through your mouth. This is your heritage as a child of God.

There's miraculous power in the Word of God. It is a seed that, once planted by faith in a human heart, will produce more blessings than one can imagine.
