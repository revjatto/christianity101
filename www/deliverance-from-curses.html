2. Humble yourself: “I renounce any pride and religious self-righteousness and any dignity that does not come from you. I have no claim on your mercy except that you died in my place.”

“I sever all contact I have ever had with the occult or with all false religion – particularly …

“I commit myself to get rid of all objects associated with the occult or false religion.”
